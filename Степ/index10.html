Preparation for step aerobics at home
1 / 6
Make sure that you are wearing high-quality shoes in which your foot does not slip and the foot does not deform.
2 / 6
Before starting your workout, warm yourself up with a warm-up for 10 minutes so that your muscles relax and your heart rate increases.
3 / 6
Put the bench on a completely flat surface and start from the most basic level of step-aerobic, gradually increase the intensity.
4 / 6
When you take a step, make sure that the foot is completely placed on the step platform.
5 / 6
Maintain an even posture throughout your workout. Keep your back straight, shoulders relaxed, and abs tense to avoid all kinds of injuries.
6 / 6
Drink water constantly, especially before training, to prevent dehydration.

Basic Step

Before starting your workout, warm up your muscles with a step in place. This should be done on the floor without using the step platform for 1-2 minutes. Then perform the exercise step - Step-touch - first springily, then - with sliding. Put your feet to each other alternately, to the rhythm of the music playing.



How to perform:


    •With one foot we climb to the hill, then we put the other foot.

    •We go down to the floor, alternately lowering our legs.

    •The exercise must be repeated for 1 minute.
V-step
This exercise perfectly strengthens the gluteal muscles in motion.




A step resembles the letter V.

How to perform:



• You need to step alternately on the step with two feet on opposite corners of the step.

• Alternate step with your feet on opposite corners of the platform, the legs move as if along the upper corners of the English letter V.

• We get down on the floor, but we have our legs closer to each other.


It needs to be repeated for a minute.

Knee up

How to perform:


•Step with one foot to the corner of the step platform (step with the right foot to the left corner and vice versa).

•We transfer the weight to this leg.

•The second leg is lifted up bent at the knee.

•The second leg is returned to the floor, the first leg is placed on the floor next to the second.

Perform 2-4 repetitions 15 times with each leg.


Note:


The movements should be as natural as possible and it is not necessary to shift to one side and twist the knee. The leg is directed straight, the body when lifting the knee tends slightly forward.


Сurl-leg

The exercise ensures the work of the quadriceps and gluteal muscles.

How to perform:



•Step with one foot to the corner of the step platform (step with the right foot to the left corner and vice versa).

• We transfer the weight to this leg with the second leg we do a sweep.

• The second leg is returned to the floor, the first leg is placed on the floor next to the second.

Do two, four repeats 15 times with each leg.

Step kick

How to perform:


• Step onto the platform with your right foot.

• The left one should be thrown forward sharply, imitating a blow (we do not lift the leg high).

Do two, four sets 10 times with each leg.


Note:


Keep the body straight, alternately lift the straightened leg forward and simultaneously bring the opposite arm forward. Technique is more important than speed.

Lift side

How to perform:


• Step aside

• Lifting a straight leg to the side by 45 ° (The supporting knee is slightly bent, the body is vertical).

Note:


When performing the exercise, keep your hands at chest level, and when performing a swing with your foot, spread out to the sides.


This is an isolating exercise for purposeful study of the middle gluteal muscle, that is, the outer part of the buttocks. Usually, all exercises on the back of the thigh are aimed at working out the gluteus maximus and biceps of the thigh.



This exercise requires 15 repetitions for each leg.

Mambo

How to perform:


• Step one forward.

• Step with the second foot in place.

• Step back with the first foot.

• Step with the second foot in place.

Note:


One leg is always in place (on the platform), and only the second leg moves back and forth and stands on the floor. This exercise improves the work of the respiratory system, develops coordination, strengthens muscles.


Lift back

How to perform:


• Take a step onto the platform.

• Pull your straight leg back.

• Take two steps to the floor.

The exercise ensures the work of the quadriceps and gluteal muscles.
